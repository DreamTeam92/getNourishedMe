ABOUT ME

Get to know me
a little better

Efficiency over all

Hi. I'm Sabina.


I founded GetNourished.Me out of a deep passion for assisting people in achieving their best well-being. I am a dedicated and passionate nutrition advisor committed to helping individuals achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized nutrition strategies. With a strong background in nutritional science and years of hands-on experience, I believe in a holistic approach to health that incorporates balanced nutrition, physical activity, and mental well-being.

I’m a qualified Nutrition Advisor with a Level 4 diploma in nutrition and weight management, Level 3 Personal Trainer, Corrective Exercises Specialist, Biomechanics Specialist, Human Foundations Practitioner, Level 3 Mat Pilates and Reform Pilates Teacher.

Health and well-being have been a part of my life since childhood. That's why I decided that I would like to work together with people, helping and motivating them through their journey.

My approach to nutrition is individualized and evidence-based. I understand that each person’s body, lifestyle, and health goals are unique, which is why I take the time to get to know my clients and develop customized nutrition plans that are both effective and sustainable. I can also create a personalized training program if required. Please visit my website to find out more.

I am passionate about helping you achieve your health goals. Let’s work together to create a healthier, happier you! ...read more.
