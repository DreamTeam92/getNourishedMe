Healthy Moms,Healthy Babies: Nutrition for Every Stage of Motherhood

Pre-natal nutrition

Focuses on the period before and during pregnancy. Proper nutrition during this time is crucial for the health of you and your baby

Balanced Diet: Encourage a well-balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats

Folic Acid: Recommend at least 400 micrograms of folic acid daily to reduce the risk of neural tube defects

Iron: Ensure adequate iron intake to prevent anemia

Healthy Weight: Promote maintaining a healthy weight to improve fertility and reduce pregnancy complications

During Pregnancy: Increased Caloric Intake especially in the second and third trimesters, but emphasize nutrient-dense foods

Key Nutrients: Highlight the importance of folic acid, iron, calcium, vitamin D, omega-3 fatty acids, and protein. Educate on avoiding certain foods like raw fish, unpasteurized cheeses, and deli meats to prevent infections

Post-Natal Nutrition

It is essential for the recovery of you as a mother and the health of the breastfeeding infant

Balanced Diet: Continue to emphasize a balanced diet to support recovery and overall health. Highlight the importance of calcium, vitamin D, and omega-3 fatty acids for milk quality
